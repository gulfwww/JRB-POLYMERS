PVC PLASTICIZER

PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) plasticizers are additives used to increase the flexibility, workability, and pliability of rigid PVC (a hard and brittle material in its natural form). Plasticizers are crucial in transforming PVC into a soft and flexible material, suitable for a variety of applications such as cables, hoses, flooring, and more. They work by embedding themselves between the polymer chains of PVC, reducing the intermolecular forces, and increasing mobility of the chains.
